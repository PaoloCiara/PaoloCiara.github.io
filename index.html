Flagship project

Networking Hub

An AI-powered career command center for macOS. It hunts openings across twelve sources, scores them against your resume, researches companies down to their SEC filings, finds people worth emailing, teaches you the role — and ships with a built-in AI assistant.

12-source opportunity feed

Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, SmartRecruiters, Workable, HN Who's Hiring, The Muse, Remotive, Google Jobs, USAJobs, Adzuna — plus an AI importer that reads any careers page.

AI resume matching

Every posting scored 0-100 against your resume with a one-line reason, in parallel batches. Strong fits trigger desktop notifications.

Built-in AI assistant

A popup chat grounded in whatever you're viewing — a lesson, your resume, a project. Save conversations and pick them back up anytime.

Company deep dives

Research briefs built from live news, Claude's web search, and SEC EDGAR filings — 10-Ks and 8-Ks surfaced for finance interview prep.

Contact intelligence

Finds shared-school alumni and recruiters at your best-fit companies, drafts personalized outreach, and reminds you when follow-ups are due.

Recruiting-cycle forecast

Predicts which roles and programs open next by combining your feed's history with known cycles — tech internships, IB summer analyst, PE on-cycle.

AI-generated courses

Full prep courses for any role — chapter-depth lessons written in one pass, quizzes, videos, flashcard review, and syllabus imports for real classes.

Resume-boosting projects

Each course includes hard, portfolio-grade projects with milestones and the exact resume line the work earns — built with the assistant at your side.
